About

Hyperloop Pod Design

Hyperloop is a futuristic transportation concept. The hyperloop pod will be traveling at high speeds in a low pressure tube utilizing magnetic levitation. Binghamton Hyperloop is bringing this concept to life.


Binghamton Hyperloop

Binghamton Hyperloop is a team of dedicated Binghamton University students working together to build a hyperloop pod. We are competing in the Hyperloop Pod Competition sponsored by SpaceX. Our team will be designing and building a scale model to compete on SpaceX's one-mile test track. We will be competing against teams across the globe to build the fastest and safest pod. It is our mission to make the design efficient, light, fast and environmentally friendly.

Join us, as we speed up the future.
